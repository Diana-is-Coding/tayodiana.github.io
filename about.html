Whats in the Name

The buisness name shows family values. When my sister was younger and we tried to teach her her name, we would tell her her first and last name, this led her to believe people had two names, She called our dad Daddy-Boy, our Mum Mummy-Yinka and me Tayo-Diana. I chose to use this name for my company because my sister means the world to me and I wanted t o link my business to a happy memory. After all, we get the best work done when we are happy!
